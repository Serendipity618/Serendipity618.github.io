## Research Interests  
I am a **machine learning researcher** specializing in **deep anomaly detection**, with a focus on **interpretability and security**. My research involves:  
- **Explainable AI**: Developing counterfactual, prototype-based, and attention-driven explanation methods.  
- **Robust Anomaly Detection**: Addressing vulnerabilities in deep learning models against adversarial and backdoor attacks.  
- **Backdoor Attacks & Defenses**: Investigating attack strategies and defensive mechanisms in anomaly detection systems.  
I am particularly interested in the application of these techniques in **log analysis, cybersecurity, and large-scale anomaly detection**.

## Research Projects  
### **Backdoor Attacks Against Deep Log Anomaly Detection Models** *(Sep 2023 - Present)*  
- Developed **backdoor attack frameworks** for **self-supervised log anomaly detection models** (DeepLog, LogBERT).  
- Proposed **BadSAD**, a clean-label backdoor attack against **DeepSAD** models for **image anomaly detection**.  
- Designed **one-class sequential backdoor attack strategies** for **Deep SVDD and OC4Seq models**.

### **Developing Inherently Interpretable Deep Anomaly Detection Models** *(Jan 2022 - Aug 2023)*  
- Designed **prototype-based explainability** for sequential anomaly detection using **contrastive learning**.  
- Proposed **GLEAD**, a globally & locally explainable anomaly detection method (IEEE Big Data 2022).  

### **Counterfactual Explanation for One-Class Deep Anomaly Detection** *(Aug 2020 - Dec 2021)*  
- Developed **CFDet**, a counterfactual explanation framework for one-class sequential anomaly detection (Deep SVDD, OC4Seq).  
- Applied CFDet to **IoT security** to reduce false alarms and enhance interpretability.
